## ✅ What's Included (Demo)

- **Hero Section** — Full-screen landing with animated headline, Mexican-modern aesthetic
- **Info Strip** — Address, phone, hours, price range, and service types at a glance
- **About Section** — Restaurant story, stats (210+ reviews, open until 2 AM, 20₾ starting price)
- **Menu Section** — 6 dishes with category tabs (Burritos, Tacos, Quesadillas, Combos)
- **Experience Section** — Opening hours, counter service info, late-night & delivery highlights
- **Reviews Section** — Real Google reviewer quotes with star ratings
- **Contact & Map** — Address, clickable phone number, embedded Google Maps
- **CTA Banner** — Call-to-action with phone number
- **Footer** — Logo, copyright, navigation links

---

## 🎨 Design

- **Style:** Modern + Mexican fusion — dark backgrounds, red, gold & blue accents
- **Fonts:** Bebas Neue (headings), Playfair Display (accents), DM Sans (body)
- **Animations:** CSS fade-in on load, IntersectionObserver scroll reveals
- **Responsive:** Mobile-friendly layout (breakpoint at 820px)
